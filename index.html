The Final Antidote

One day at school, I noticed that everybody was sick. Everyone was coughing, queasy and tired except for my best friend, who was just eating his french fries like normal. I thought that it was just the flu, but little did I know about the events that were about to unfold.

What should I do?

Option a: Go home and forget about it

Option b: Warn the nurse about all the stick students

Option c: Stay in school and do nothing
